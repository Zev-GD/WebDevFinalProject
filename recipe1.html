Chocolate Chip Muffins

instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and grease muffin tin
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg
  3. Whisk together butter, oil, sugar, and eggs until combined, then add sour cream, milk and vanilla
  4. Fill muffin tins and sprinkle cane sugar on top of each muffin
  5. Bake at 425°F for 5 minutes, then decrease temperature to 350°F and bake for another 25-26 minutes
  6. Let muffins cool for 10 minutes
