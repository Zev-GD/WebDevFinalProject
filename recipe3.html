Shakshuka

Ingredients:

instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat in a large skillet
  2. Add onion and red peppers, and cook for 5-8 minutes
  3. Add garlic, paprika, cumin, and cayenne, and stir until fragrant
  4. Add tomatoes, salt, and pepper, and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring frequently
  5. Create 6 wells in the sauce, and crack an egg into each of them
  6. Cover the pan and let cook for 4-8 minutes, or until the eggs are cooked to your liking
  7. Top with parsley and feta, serve with pita
