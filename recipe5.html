Chana Masala

Ingredients:

instructions:

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat
  2. Once the pan is hot, add oil, cumin seeds, and onion
  3. Sauté for 7-8 minutes, add 1-2 tablespoons water if the mixture gets too dry
  4. Add garlic, ginger, and green chili pepper, and sauté for another 2-3 minutes
  5. Mix in the chickpeas, salt, and 2 cups of water
  6. Raise heat to high, bringing mixture to a boil, then lower to medium and cook for 25-30 minutes
  7. Uncover and raise to high heat
  8. Use spoon to slightly crush the chickpeas, and sauté for another 2-3 minutes
  9. Turn off heat and add garam masala, lemon juice, and cilantro leaves
  10. Top with julienned ginger and black pepper
