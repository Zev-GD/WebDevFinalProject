Butternut Squash Mac n Cheese

From Sally's Baking Addiction

Ingredients:

instructions:

  1. Add squash, broth, milk, and garlic in a pan over medium-high heat
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 20 minutes
  3. Preheat oven to 375°F
  4. Boil water for the pasta, cook the pasta until al dante, then add the kale and cook for another 1-2 minutes
  5. Drain the pasta and kale
  6. Put squash mixture in a blender, add yogurt, salt, pepper, and nutmeg, and blend until combined
  7. Stir cheese into sauce
  8. Combine sauce with pasta and kale
  9. Pour into a 9x13 inch pan and top with breadcrumbs and more cheese
  10. Cover with tinfoil and cook for 20 minutes, then remove tinfoil and cook for another 5 minutes
