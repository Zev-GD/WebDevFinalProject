Molasses Cookies

instructions:

  1. Whisk together flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, nutmef, and salt
  2. With a mixer, mix together butter and both sugars on high speed until smooth
  3. Add molasses and mix until combined
  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract and mix until combined
  5. On low speed, add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until combined
  6. Cover dough with foil or plastic wrap and refridgerate for 1 hour
  7. Preheat oven to 350°F
  8. Line two large cookie sheets with parchment paper
  9. Roll cookie dough into balls, each containing about 1 tablespoon of dough
  10. Roll each ball in cane sugar
  11. Bake for 11-12 minutes
