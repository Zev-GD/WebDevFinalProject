instructions:

  1. To make dressing, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, and shallot until it forms a thick mixture
  2. Preheat oven to 400°F
  3. Put sweet potato on a sheet pan, coat with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper
  4. Bake sweet potato for 20 minutes
  5. Put kale in a large salad bowl and mix it with half of the dressing
  6. Top with sweet potato, quinoa, pomegranite seeds, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, hemp seeds, and apple
  7. Toss to combine and drizzle with additional dressing
