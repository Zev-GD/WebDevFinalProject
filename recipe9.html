instructions:

  1. Prepare ice bath for eggs
  2. Boil water, reduce to medium heat, and put eggs in water
  3. Lower heat to simmer and cook for 7 minutes
  4. Transfer eggs to ice bath for 5 minutes
  5. Boil broth
  6. Add noodles and cook for 1 minute
  7. Add ginger, garlic, and bok choy, and cook for 2 minutes
  8. Remove from heat and add soy sauce and sesame oil
  9. Peel eggs and cut them in half
  10. Top with bok choy, eggs, siracha, and green onions
